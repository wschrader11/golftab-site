Messaging Terms

Effective Date: May 4, 2026  ·  Last Updated: May 4, 2026

Short version: Golf Tab sends one transactional SMS per completed round to participating players. Reply STOP to opt out. Reply HELP for help. Message and data rates may apply.

1. Program Name

Brand name: Golf Tab

2. Program Description

Transactional SMS notifications delivering golf round results to participating players. Messages are sent only after a round in which the recipient participated has been completed and the round scorer has submitted final results.

3. Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Check with your mobile carrier for details on the rates that apply to your plan.

4. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. You will receive one message per completed round in which you participated.

5. Customer Support

For help, reply HELP or contact support@golftab.app.

You can also reach our support team Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM Eastern Time. Typical response time is 1–2 business days.

6. Opt-Out Instructions

To stop receiving messages, reply STOP. You will receive a confirmation and no further messages.

For help at any time, reply HELP. You will receive a message with support contact information and a link to these Messaging Terms.

7. Privacy Policy

Mobile phone numbers and SMS messaging data are handled in accordance with our Messaging Privacy Policy. We do not sell or share mobile data with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes. See also our general Privacy Policy.

8. Carrier Liability

Carriers are not liable for any delayed or undelivered messages.

9. Eligibility and Consent

To receive messages from Golf Tab, you must be a participant in a Golf Tab round whose mobile number was added by the round scorer (with consent confirmation shown to the scorer at the time the number was entered), or you must have provided your own number in the Golf Tab app and enabled SMS notifications. You represent that you are the subscriber or authorized user of the mobile number you provide.

10. Supported Carriers

The Golf Tab messaging program is available on most major U.S. mobile carriers. Supported carriers may change without notice. Carriers are not liable for any delayed or undelivered messages.

11. Changes to These Terms

We may update these Messaging Terms from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page and revise the "Last Updated" date above. Material changes will be communicated as required by applicable law.
